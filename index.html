2020

Subli+ plans on opening a smaller office in San Pedro Sula, the second largest city of Honduras, with the purpose of expanding their operations.

2018

Subli+ begins to produce their clothing line starting with simple t-shirts, later on expanding to jerseys, jackets, and other outerware.

2016

More machinery is bought allowing them to create custom-printed household items such as bags,
thermoses, etc. Also, 2016 was the year that Subli+ began donating 15% of their net profit to help fight poverty in Honduras.

2014

Subli+ begins operations with only a couple machines, the original purpose was to solely produce stickers.
